Help - Video Player

Adding/Removing Videos

Add videos to the player via Drag and Drop,
or by clicking Project ↪ Load ↪ Video

To remove a loaded video,
click the X - button located in its top right corner.

Navigation Bar

/- Buttons: Play/Pause
/- Buttons: Skip a single frame
/- Buttons: Skip a fixed number of frames (default: 10)
Project ↪ Settings to change
