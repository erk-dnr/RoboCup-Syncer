Help - Editing

For each video, a color-coded track bar will appear,
allowing you to mark down all parts to be included in the final cut.

Usage/Shortcuts

On a track bar, hold left click and drag your mouse
to mark the corresponding time span (displayed above).

Once a marker is set, it can be resized by clicking near the respective end
and dragging it to the desired position.

To fine adjust your resize, move the mouse to the very end of a marker
until the ↔ - Symbol appears.

Double click a marker to delete it.
